BRUSSELS, Aug. 21, 2001 (paperloop.com) - Sappi has restarted PM 1 at its Stanger mill in South Africa following a rebuild to improve product quality and boost the capacity.  The 61,000 tonne/yr PM's uncoated woodfree capacity is expected to reach 85,000 tonnes/yr by the end of the year.
